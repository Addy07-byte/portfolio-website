About Me

I’m Aditya Hede, a Full-Stack Developer with deep expertise in Cloud Deployment and Applied AI. I design and build end-to-end solutions — from intuitive frontends to resilient backends — that scale seamlessly on the cloud.

With a Master’s in Information Technology from Arizona State University, my experience spans AWS, CI/CD, Infrastructure as Code, DevOps, and Machine Learning. My development philosophy blends clean, maintainable code with automation, observability, and data-driven decision-making. Whether deploying microservices, integrating APIs, or training models, I focus on delivering solutions that are production-ready and impactful.
